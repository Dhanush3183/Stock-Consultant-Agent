Frequently Asked Questions

When is the best time to invest?

The best time to invest is when you have a clear financial goal and can afford to invest without compromising your emergency fund.

How much should I invest?

A general rule is to invest 10-20% of your income after covering essential expenses and building an emergency fund.

Should I invest in individual stocks or mutual funds?

Beginners often benefit from mutual funds or ETFs for instant diversification, while individual stocks require more research and risk tolerance.
